Add `TimePicker` and `DateTimePicker` components. `TimePicker` is a 24-hour time picker that can show or hide a seconds column (`HH:MM` or `HH:MM:SS`), with a configurable minute/second `step`. `DateTimePicker` combines a calendar and time selector in a single bordered trigger, yielding one `Date` with both day and time. The shared inline `TimeScroller` and `formatTime` helper are also exported.
